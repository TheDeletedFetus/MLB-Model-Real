====================================================
FEATURE TESTING METHODOLOGY
====================================================

For each feature:

1. Determine which team held the advantage.
2. Generate a feature-only pick.
3. Compare against actual winner.
4. Record:
   - Games
   - Correct
   - Incorrect
   - Win %
   - Market Expected Win %
   - Delta vs Market
   - ROI

Signal Classification:

55%+
Potential Strong Signal

53%-55%
Potential Positive Signal

50%-53%
Possible Weak Signal

48%-50%
Likely Noise

Below 48%
Potential Negative Signal

====================================================
LESSONS LEARNED
====================================================

1. Backtesting before feature testing can hide weak signals.
2. Confidence scores are meaningless until calibrated.
3. More complexity does not guarantee more accuracy.
4. Preserve all historical features.
5. Signal discovery precedes weight optimization.
6. Win percentage alone does not equal betting edge.
7. Market comparison is ultimately required.
8. Every new feature should automatically enter the testing pipeline.

====================================================
CURRENT DEVELOPMENT RULE
====================================================

DO NOT ADD OR REMOVE WEIGHTS BASED ON OPINION.

All future model changes should be supported by:

- Feature testing
- Market comparison
- Out-of-sample validation
- Historical evidence
